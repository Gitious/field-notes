**Intro (0:00)**
What if your phone could be an autonomous research assistant? Meet Field Notes – your AI agent for effortless field research.

**SCENE 1: Phone Camera View (0:05)**
Here, we see Field Notes in action, streaming live video from a phone camera. Using the **Gemini Live API with function calling**, our agent autonomously observes its surroundings in real-time. Notice how it instantly processes the visual input, generating an observation: "A wide, open-plan workspace..." This isn't just transcription; it's intelligent, *autonomous* perception, ready for analysis.

**SCENE 2: Dashboard Timeline (0:35)**
Switching to the dashboard, the Timeline view showcases Field Notes' *autonomous* organization. Each card is a detailed observation, automatically categorized and tagged. The agent leverages **Senso Context OS** for persistent memory, building a rich, structured understanding of your environment. On the right, the "Autonomous Activity" feed logs every AI response and sync, demonstrating the agent's continuous, self-driven operation. For deeper insights, Field Notes can even use **Tavily** for web enrichment.

**SCENE 3: Dashboard Chat (1:05)**
The Chat interface, powered by **assistant-ui**, allows natural language interaction with your agent's memory. Here, I simply ask "what did I observe last session?" and Field Notes autonomously retrieves and summarizes relevant observations from its persistent memory, proving its capability to recall and synthesize information without manual searching.

**SCENE 4: Dashboard Report (1:30)**
Field Notes doesn't just observe; it reports. With a single click, it *autonomously* generates a comprehensive field research report. This isn't a template; it’s a dynamic document summarizing all observations, key findings, and an executive summary, saving countless hours of manual compilation.

**SCENE 5: Dashboard Stats (1:50)**
Finally, the Stats view provides an overview of your research, showing total observations, sessions, and category distribution. All this meticulously collected and organized data can be effortlessly exported via **Nexla Express.Dev**, integrating seamlessly into your existing workflows. Field Notes: truly autonomous, truly intelligent, truly transformative field research.
